§ 03 — TYPOGRAPHY
Fraunces & Inter.
A warm, contemporary serif with real personality — paired with a geometric sans that stays out of its way. Distinctive without being loud.
Fraunces Display · Variable serif
Weights 300 / 400 / 600 / 700
Italics: 300 / 400 / 600
Used for: titles, pull-quotes, numerals
Inter Body · Geometric sans
Weights 300–700
Used for: body, kickers, captions, labels
Noto Serif / Sans JP Japanese parallel
Modern mincho + gothic
Used for: JP display + body
JetBrains Mono Monospace (sparingly)
Used for: page numbers, hex codes, metadata
Ag Ag
A quiet revolution, precisely measured.
静かな革新を、正確に記す。
The pairing works because neither font tries to win the argument. Fraunces brings warmth and quirk to every heading; Inter gives body copy room to be read. Japanese parallels sit naturally because Noto's proportions are modernist, not historical.
Fraunces + Inter + Noto — a system
